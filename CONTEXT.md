# Jataí

Jataí is an educational developer platform for learning and prototyping Open Finance integrations in a safe, simulated environment.

## Language

**Jataí**:
The product and brand name, always written in title case with the accented final í.
_Avoid_: JATAÍ, Jatai, jataí

**Jataí SDK**:
The single educational SDK through which learners and developers access Jataí's simulated Open Finance capabilities.
_Avoid_: Product suite, collection of separate tools

**Module**:
A focused capability accessed through the Jataí SDK, such as accounts, payments, authorization, telemetry, or synthetic data.
_Avoid_: Standalone product

**Mel**:
Jataí's bee mascot and AI learning companion. Mel explains concepts, interprets SDK and sandbox errors, offers contextual hints, and recommends the learner's next step without completing exercises on their behalf.
_Avoid_: General-purpose chatbot, autonomous coding agent

**Learner**:
A student or junior developer who understands basic JavaScript or TypeScript but is new to Open Finance concepts and integrations.
_Avoid_: Complete programming beginner, financial-integration expert

**Product Concept**:
The fictional academic product represented by the Jataí website. It communicates a credible SDK and learning experience but is not a real financial service or production platform.
_Avoid_: Live financial product, regulated service

**First Flight**:
The flagship 15-minute learning journey in which a learner uses the fictional SDK to create consent, retrieve a synthetic account, initiate a simulated Pix payment, and inspect its callback with guidance from Mel.
_Avoid_: Hello World, production transaction

**Interactive Demo**:
The front-end-only representation of the fictional Jataí product. SDK responses, the First Flight, and Mel's guidance are scripted locally and require no backend, live financial API, authentication service, or language model.
_Avoid_: MVP backend, production sandbox
